About

Bob's Car Rental was founded in 1976 in Oakville, Ontario by a young entrepreneur fresh out of Harvard Business School. Since opening his first shop, Bob Stevenson’s company has expanded to over 50 locations in Ontario. What separates Bob’s Car Rental from other car rental businesses is their excellent customer service. Bob’s Car Rental employees are required to smile when dealing with customers, and refer to all customers as either “sir” or “ma’am”, to make the customers feel properly respected in the store. This has been the secret to Bob Stevenson's successful business model.
